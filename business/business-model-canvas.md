## Customer Segments
- Small to medium-sized businesses (SMBs) with 50-500 employees
- Tech startups and scaleups with high knowledge turnover
- Consulting firms and agencies with distributed teams
- Companies in regulated industries (e.g., finance, healthcare) requiring knowledge documentation

## Value Propositions
- Reduce knowledge silos and employee dependency risks
- Centralized knowledge base with AI-powered search and recommendations
- Automated knowledge documentation and sharing workflows
- Real-time knowledge gap analysis and reporting
- Seamless integration with existing collaboration tools (e.g., Slack, Microsoft Teams)

## Channels
- Direct sales via company website and sales team
- Partnerships with business consultants and IT service providers
- Online marketing through content marketing, SEO, and targeted ads
- Webinars and demo sessions for potential customers
- Referral programs and customer success stories

## Customer Relationships
- Dedicated customer success managers for enterprise clients
- Self-service knowledge base and community forums
- Regular check-ins and health checks for SMBs
- 24/7 customer support via chat, email, and phone
- Annual user conferences and networking events

## Revenue Streams
- Subscription-based pricing tiers (Basic: $20/user/month, Pro: $50/user/month, Enterprise: Custom)
- One-time implementation and onboarding fees (Basic: $500, Pro: $2,000, Enterprise: Custom)
- Premium support and consulting services (Hourly rate: $150/hour)
- Add-on modules and integrations (e.g., advanced analytics, custom integrations)
- Annual maintenance and update fees (15% of the subscription cost)
