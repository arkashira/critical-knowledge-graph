# Marketing Plan

## Positioning
**Primary:** "The ultimate knowledge management platform for SMBs to reduce employee dependency and ensure seamless knowledge sharing."
**Alternatives:**
1. "Empower your team with critical knowledge graph to eliminate bottlenecks."
2. "Transform your business with a robust knowledge management system that ensures continuity."
3. "Unlock the power of collective knowledge with our advanced dependency reduction platform."

## Ideal Customer Profiles (ICPs)
1. **Sarah, the SMB Operations Manager**
   - **Daily Job:** Manages day-to-day operations, ensures smooth workflow, and handles employee onboarding/offboarding.
   - **Budget:** $500 - $1,000/month

2. **Mark, the IT Director**
   - **Daily Job:** Oversees IT infrastructure, ensures data security, and manages knowledge bases.
   - **Budget:** $1,000 - $2,000/month

3. **Lisa, the HR Manager**
   - **Daily Job:** Handles employee training, onboarding, and knowledge transfer.
   - **Budget:** $300 - $800/month

## Channels
1. **LinkedIn Ads**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $50 - $100 per acquisition
   - **Rationale:** Targets professionals in operations, IT, and HR roles.

2. **Google Ads**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $30 - $70 per acquisition
   - **Rationale:** Captures search intent for knowledge management solutions.

3. **Content Marketing (Blog, Webinars)**
   - **CAC Estimate:** $20 - $50 per acquisition
   - **Rationale:** Builds thought leadership and attracts organic traffic.

## Content Cadence
- **Week 1-4:** Blog posts on knowledge management best practices, case studies, and webinars.
- **Week 5-8:** LinkedIn articles, guest posts on industry blogs, and email campaigns.
- **Week 9-12:** Customer testimonials, success stories, and follow-up webinars.

## Launch Milestones
- **D-30:** Finalize marketing collateral, set up ad campaigns, and prepare launch webinar.
- **D-0:** Official product launch, press release, and social media blitz.
- **D+30:** First customer testimonials, initial performance review, and ad optimization.
- **D+90:** Comprehensive performance analysis, customer feedback integration, and scaling strategies.

## Success Metrics
- **DAU (Daily Active Users):** Target 500 DAU by D+90.
- **$MRR Target:** Achieve $10,000 MRR by D+90.
