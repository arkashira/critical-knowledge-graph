# User Stories

## Epic 1: Knowledge Capture and Documentation

**Story 1: Knowledge Capture**
- **As a** team lead,
- **I want** to capture and document critical system knowledge,
- **So that** I can ensure that important information is not lost when team members leave.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should allow for the creation of knowledge entries.
- Each entry should include metadata such as author, date, and relevance.
- The system should support rich text formatting for detailed documentation.
- Entries should be searchable by keywords and metadata.
- **Complexity:** M

**Story 2: Knowledge Tagging**
- **As a** knowledge manager,
- **I want** to tag knowledge entries with relevant categories and keywords,
- **So that** I can easily organize and retrieve information.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should support tagging of knowledge entries.
- Tags should be hierarchical and customizable.
- The system should suggest tags based on existing entries.
- Tags should be searchable and filterable.
- **Complexity:** S

## Epic 2: Knowledge Sharing and Collaboration

**Story 3: Knowledge Sharing**
- **As a** team member,
- **I want** to share knowledge entries with specific team members or groups,
- **So that** I can ensure that the right people have access to the information they need.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should allow for sharing knowledge entries with specific users or groups.
- Shared entries should be visible in the recipient's knowledge feed.
- The system should support different levels of access (view, edit, comment).
- Shared entries should be trackable for audit purposes.
- **Complexity:** M

**Story 4: Collaboration on Knowledge Entries**
- **As a** team member,
- **I want** to collaborate on knowledge entries with my colleagues,
- **So that** we can collectively improve the quality and accuracy of the information.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should support real-time collaboration on knowledge entries.
- Changes should be tracked and visible to all collaborators.
- The system should support comments and discussions on entries.
- Collaborators should be notified of changes and comments.
- **Complexity:** L

## Epic 3: Knowledge Retrieval and Utilization

**Story 5: Knowledge Search**
- **As a** team member,
- **I want** to search for knowledge entries using keywords and filters,
- **So that** I can quickly find the information I need.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should support full-text search across all knowledge entries.
- Search results should be sortable and filterable by metadata.
- The system should support advanced search features such as boolean operators.
- Search results should be ranked by relevance.
- **Complexity:** M

**Story 6: Knowledge Recommendations**
- **As a** team member,
- **I want** to receive recommendations for relevant knowledge entries,
- **So that** I can stay informed about important information.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should analyze my activity and provide personalized recommendations.
- Recommendations should be based on my role, past searches, and interactions.
- The system should support manual curation of recommendations by knowledge managers.
- Recommendations should be visible in my knowledge feed.
- **Complexity:** L

## Epic 4: Knowledge Management and Analytics

**Story 7: Knowledge Analytics**
- **As a** knowledge manager,
- **I want** to analyze the usage and impact of knowledge entries,
- **So that** I can identify gaps and areas for improvement.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should track metrics such as views, shares, and comments for each entry.
- The system should provide dashboards and reports on knowledge usage.
- The system should support customizable analytics and reporting.
- The system should alert me to entries with low engagement.
- **Complexity:** L

**Story 8: Knowledge Review and Approval**
- **As a** knowledge manager,
- **I want** to review and approve knowledge entries before they are shared,
- **So that** I can ensure the quality and accuracy of the information.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should support a workflow for review and approval of knowledge entries.
- Reviewers should be notified of new entries requiring review.
- The system should track the status of each entry (draft, under review, approved, rejected).
- Approved entries should be automatically shared with the relevant team members.
- **Complexity:** M

**Story 9: Knowledge Retirement**
- **As a** knowledge manager,
- **I want** to retire outdated or irrelevant knowledge entries,
- **So that** I can maintain the relevance and accuracy of the knowledge base.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should support a workflow for retiring knowledge entries.
- Retired entries should be archived and no longer visible in search results.
- The system should track the retirement history of each entry.
- Retired entries should be recoverable if needed.
- **Complexity:** S

**Story 10: Knowledge Backup and Export**
- **As a** knowledge manager,
- **I want** to backup and export knowledge entries,
- **So that** I can ensure data integrity and compliance.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
- The system should support automated backups of the knowledge base.
- The system should allow for manual export of knowledge entries in various formats (PDF, CSV, etc.).
- Exported entries should retain their formatting and metadata.
- The system should support encryption and secure storage of backups.
- **Complexity:** M
