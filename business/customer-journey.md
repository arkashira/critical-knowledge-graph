# customer-journey.md

## **Aware**  
- **Trigger event**: Key employee leaves without documenting critical systems/processes, causing operational delays.  
- **Friction points**:  
  - Belief that "knowledge loss is inevitable" or "we’ll fix it later."  
  - Underestimating the cost of employee dependency (e.g., 6–12 months to replace a skilled worker).  
- **User emotions**: Panic, frustration, helplessness.  
- **Opportunity to delight**:  
  - Highlight **preventive value** (e.g., "Avoid 30%+ revenue loss from knowledge gaps").  
  - Share a **1-click demo** showing instant knowledge capture from emails/Slack.  
- **Metric**: Website visits → Demo requests (target: 15% conversion).  

---

## **Consider**  
- **Trigger event**: Decision-makers compare KM tools (e.g., Confluence, Notion) but find them too generic.  
- **Friction points**:  
  - Overwhelmed by feature bloat in competitors.  
  - Skepticism about ROI for SMEs ("Is this worth $500/month?").  
- **User emotions**: Skepticism, hesitation, analysis paralysis.  
- **Opportunity to delight**:  
  - **ROI calculator** (input: # of employees, turnover rate → output: annual savings).  
  - Case study: "Local logistics co. reduced onboarding time from 6 weeks to 3 days."  
- **Metric**: Time-on-page for pricing page (>3 mins = qualified lead).  

---

## **Try**  
- **Trigger event**: Signs up for free trial after a 1:1 consult.  
- **Friction points**:  
  - Setup friction (e.g., integrating with existing tools like Jira/Google Workspace).  
  - Team resistance ("Another tool? We’re busy!").  
- **User emotions**: Cautious optimism, mild frustration.  
- **Opportunity to delight**:  
  - **Zero-config onboarding**: Auto-imports knowledge from 5+ common platforms.  
  - **Gamified adoption**: Badges for "first 10 docs created" to boost team engagement.  
- **Metric**: Trial-to-paid conversion rate (target: 25% in 14 days).  

---

## **Adopt**  
- **Trigger event**: Teams use the platform for 30+ days, automating knowledge capture.  
- **Friction points**:  
  - Inconsistent documentation habits ("Who’s responsible for updating this?").  
  - Overlooking advanced features (e.g., AI-generated dependency maps).  
- **User emotions**: Relief, growing confidence.  
- **Opportunity to delight**:  
  - **Proactive alerts**: "Jane’s leaving next week—review her 12 critical docs."  
  - **AI co-pilot**: Auto-summarizes meeting notes into actionable knowledge.  
- **Metric**: Daily active users (DAU) > 70% of licensed seats.  

---

## **Expand**  
- **Trigger event**: Leadership sees reduced turnover costs and requests scaling.  
- **Friction points**:  
  - Budget constraints for enterprise plans.  
  - Need for custom features (e.g., compliance reporting).  
- **User emotions**: Satisfaction, loyalty.  
- **Opportunity to delight**:  
  - **Tiered upsells**: "Add 50% more users for 20% discount."  
  - **Ecosystem integrations**: Connect to HRIS systems for automated onboarding/offboarding.  
- **Metric**: Cross-sell/upsell rate (target: 40% of adopters expand within 6 months).  

--- 

**Total journey metric**: Net Promoter Score (NPS) > 60 (tracked via post-trial surveys).
